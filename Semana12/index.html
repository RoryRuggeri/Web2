Welcome to the Archives!

Letter n' Type is a Collaborative Digital Type Foundry. Since 2011 Lost Type has been a source for unique typefaces, with a collection of over 50 different faces from contributors all over the world. 100% of the funds from sales of these fonts go directly to their respective designers. Letter n' Type fonts have been used across the world, and recently have been featured in projects for Nike, Starbucks and Coca-Cola. The site continues to be dedicated to to the idea that quality fonts should be made available to anyone who wants to create an individual piece of characterful design.

New featured fonts

Geared is an industrial inspired Condensed Slab Serif in 4 weights (Thin, Regular, Bold, Extrabold). With an extensive character set, Geared could be a versatile addition to your next project.

Matchbook is Simon Walker's first typeface release, evolving from lettering drawn for sub-headings in his own pieces. Its extended forms and rounded serifs create a characterful and unique tone in headlines.

Moriston is a sans-serif grotesque available in 6 reliable weights, with support for over 200 languages and a variety of additional number styles. Contrast to a conversation makes your voice stand out.
